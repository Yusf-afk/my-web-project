Need Help?

We're here to guide you. Here are some frequently asked questions and useful tips to get the most out of Badalni.

How do I add an item?

Go to the "Add Item" page, fill out the form with your item’s name, description, photo, and your contact info. Then click "Post Item." That’s it!

Can I trade more than one item?

Yes! You can add as many items as you want. Each one will show up on your profile.

How do I contact someone for a trade?

Visit the item page and you'll see the owner’s contact info and trade options. You can message or call them directly.

I made a mistake on my item post. What do I do?

Go to your profile, find the item, and click "Edit" or "Delete." You’re in control.

Need more help?

Contact us at support@badalni.tn or send us a message through the Contact page.
